Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect
As a water bottle manufacturing company, we collect personal and business information such as your name, company name, contact number, email address, billing and shipping details when you place bulk orders, request quotations, or contact us. We may also collect technical data such as IP address, browser type, and website usage patterns to enhance user experience.
2. How We Use Your Information
The information collected is used to process orders, manage bulk manufacturing requests, provide quotations, arrange logistics and delivery, and offer customer support. We may also use your contact details to share product updates, promotional offers, or important service-related communications.
3. Data Protection and Security
We implement strict security measures to safeguard your personal and business data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. This includes secure servers, restricted data access, and regular monitoring of our systems to ensure data integrity and confidentiality.
4. Sharing of Information
We do not sell or trade your personal or business information. However, we may share necessary data with trusted third parties such as raw material suppliers, logistics partners, and payment gateways strictly for order fulfillment and operational purposes, under confidentiality agreements.
5. Your Rights and Choices
You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You may also opt out of marketing communications at any time. For any privacy-related concerns or requests, you can contact our support team directly.
